WELCOME TO AL HAYA HOSPITAL

Al-Haya Egyptian Hospital was established more than 50 years ago and has more than 30 branches at the level of the Republic.

Working in Al-Haya Hospital, a group of best and competent doctors aim to provide the highest level of efficiency in the treatment and satisfaction of our patients.

Al Haya Hospital provides the best modern devices and methods of treatment in all specializations such as orthopedics, cardiology, pectoral diseases, tumors, interior and dermatology, as well as all aspects of children.
